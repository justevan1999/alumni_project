Are you ready to be with us????

The Younergy Pop & Sip Dance Challenge was an initiative birthed by the Bullet Energy Drink brand in a bid to distill the philosophy behind “Younergy”.

The contest was targeted at consumers in the content creation and dance/media spaces with a call to tap into the challenge to show off their Younergy through dance.

To kickstart the contest, a ‘call for entries’ post was put out on all BULLET social media platforms to notify the general public about the contest. The singular criteria for winning was to get the highest number of likes on one’s entry video as a participant.

Contestants were required to download the official instrumental/soundtrack, study the dance moves & do their best to recreate the moves as seen in the instructional video, or even create their own unique dance routines.

Contestants were then required to post their entry videos on their social media pages and tag BULLET to their entries while using the #YounergyPopAndSipDanceChallenge hashtag.

Finally, contestants were required to proceed to the previously posted ‘call for entries’ post on the Bullet Nigeria page, comment ‘done’ in the comments section and finally tag 5 of their friends in order to qualify as a valid entry into the contest.

The dance challenge ran from the 3rd till the 30th of October, after which 3 winners, @dancedivacrew, @pswag__ & @oluwadejii_ emerged as winners, taking home the much coveted prizes of N100K, N75K & N50K respectively. After all was said and done, all participants were appreciated.
